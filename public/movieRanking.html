My favorite movies

My top 5 movies

Climax

This film is my favorite because of its unique visual style and intense atmosphere, also the choreography is mesmerizing and it keeps giving me chills every time I watch it.

Anihilation

Stunning visuals, troubled characters, and haunting atmosphere. The way it explores concepts of self-destruction and transformation is both captivating and unsettling.

amelie

Whimsical and visually stunning, this film is a delightful exploration of love and how odd humans are.

saltburn

Great criticism of wealth and privilege, kinda gross at times but in a very unnerving way. The performances are top-notch, and the cinematography is absolutely stunning.

hotel budapest

Visually delightful and intricate story, you finish the movie feeling as melancholic about this place as the characters.
